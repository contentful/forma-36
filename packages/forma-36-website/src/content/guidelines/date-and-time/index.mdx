# Date and time

## Absolute vs. relative

Consider the context when choosing between absolute and relative data and time.

      <TableCell style={{ fontWeight: 700 }}>13 Aug 2019 at 10:00 am</TableCell>
      <TableCell>
        When is an entry scheduled to be published?
        <br />
        The use of relative time is not appropriate because it is not specific.
      </TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Relative</TableCell>
      <TableCell style={{ fontWeight: 700 }}>

      </TableCell>
      <TableCell>
        When was my collaborator last active?
        <br />
        The use of absolute date and time is unnecessarily specific. Provide the
        absolute date and time on user interaction, e.g. when the user hovers
        over relative time with their cursor.
      </TableCell>
    </TableRow>
  </TableBody>
</Table>

## Ranges

To show a date or time range, use an **en dash** (Unicode: U+2013, html: `&ndash;`) with spaces around it.

<Table className="f36-margin-bottom--xl" style={{ width: '100%' }}>
  <TableHead>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell style={{ width: '25%' }}>Type</TableCell>
      <TableCell style={{ width: '25%' }}>Examples</TableCell>
      <TableCell style={{ width: '50%' }}>Notes</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
  </TableHead>
  <TableBody>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Time</TableCell>
      <TableCell style={{ fontWeight: 700 }}>8:00 am – 2:00 pm</TableCell>
      <TableCell></TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Date</TableCell>
      <TableCell style={{ fontWeight: 700 }}>
        30 Mar – 30 Apr
        <br />
        8 Jul – 13 Aug 2019
        <br />
        13 Dec 2018 – 8 Aug 2019
      </TableCell>
      <TableCell>
        Omit the year on the left side if the range is in the same year. Omit it
        completely if the year can be understood from the context.
      </TableCell>
    </TableRow>
  </TableBody>
</Table>

      </TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Absolute time or date</TableCell>
      <TableCell style={{ fontWeight: 700 }}>
        08:00
        <br />

      </TableCell>
      <TableCell>
        Don't use the leading zero in single-digit hours or days.
      </TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Absolute date</TableCell>
      <TableCell style={{ fontWeight: 700 }}>4th Jan 2019</TableCell>
      <TableCell>

      </TableCell>
    </TableRow>
